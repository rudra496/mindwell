## Primary Sources

### National Institute of Mental Health (NIMH)
- **Website:** https://www.nimh.nih.gov
- **Used for:** Disorder prevalence statistics, symptom information, treatment options
- **Reliability:** Official US government agency, gold standard for mental health research
- **Citations:**
  - Mental Health Disorder Statistics: https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/statistics
  - Major Depression: https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/depression
  - Anxiety Disorders: https://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/anxiety-disorders

### World Health Organization (WHO)
- **Website:** https://www.who.int/health-topics/mental-health
- **Used for:** Global mental health statistics, international perspectives
- **Reliability:** Primary international health authority
- **Citations:**
  - Mental Health: https://www.who.int/news-room/fact-sheets/detail/mental-disorders
  - Depression Fact Sheet: https://www.who.int/news-room/fact-sheets/detail/depression

- **Citations:**
